Gold Price Data Analysis
Overview

This project looks at historical gold price data to understand how prices change over time. The aim is to identify trends, patterns, and important movements that can help in understanding gold as an investment.

Dataset

Historical gold price data

Includes date-wise gold prices over multiple years

Tools Used

Python

Jupyter Notebook

Pandas, NumPy

Matplotlib

What I Analyzed

Overall price trend over time

Periods of major rise and fall

Price movement patterns

Key Observations

Gold prices show a long-term upward trend

Prices fluctuate during economic uncertainty

Short-term volatility is common, but long-term growth is visible

Conclusion

This analysis provides a clear view of how gold prices behave over time and highlights why gold is often considered a stable long-term investment.
